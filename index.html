Odin Recipes

Odin Recipes is a website that consist of voluntary renowned chefs recipes. Mostly these chefs advocate healthy eating lifestyle. Their repasts ususally concentreate on the three course meal; made up of an appetizer, main dish and dessert. Eating in courses allows you to eat a variety of foods with a combination of flavors and nutrients.

Odin Recipes are categorized into three phases:

  • Kruz Recipes
  • Leo Recipes
  • Rene Frank Recipes
